We’d like to introduce EMEAT, a web and mobile market data solution with historical and current pricing and supply & demand data of U.S. meats.

EMEAT has been built with three key objectives in mind: i) ease of use, ii) affordability for all, and iii) no long-term commitments.

EMEAT's current features include:

Proteins and range and frequency of data

  • Up to 6 years of daily and weekly pricing and supply & demand data for Beef and Pork. The same functionality will be added for Chicken and Turkey in the third quarter of 2021.

Visualization options

  • Tables with daily and weekly data
  • Interactive volume and price charts with cyclical and continuous views

Easy analytics

  • Ability to save favorite tables and charts
  • Price alerts
  • Customizable Daily Bulletin
  • Ability to export to Excel

Other features

  • Histograms
  • Time series price comparisons
  • Min/max charts

Platforms

  • Web
  • Web responsive for mobile devices
  • Android and iOS

And many new features are in the making, including advanced analytics for forecasting and risk management.
